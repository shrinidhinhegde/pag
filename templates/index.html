Purpose

To better server the Diplomatic Community (DC) resident in Ghana, DiplomaticXpress is a platform for Diplomatic Missions and International Organisations (IOs) to action all matters related to the acquisition and maintenance of and or property granted in usufruct in Ghana. This include the due diligence actions and queries pertaining to outright purchase/assignment or short/long term leases of:
  • Terms and renewal of property leasing
  • State-owned land or property
  • Privately-owned land or property (including Stool lands)

Benefits

  • Prevents costly uncertainties
  • Eliminate the need of the DC to deal with middlemen
  • Create a smooth relationship between the Ghana Lands Commission, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Regional Integration and the Diplomatic Community.
  • A centralized depository of all mission records, files and documents related to land and property matters
